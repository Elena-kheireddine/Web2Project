Our Aerial Yoga is for any and all levels. This class takes the physical part of yoga and combines it with a silk hammock to support you through various poses. Aerial gives you the chance to explore poses that might not be accessible on the mat and can help you explore inversions with the full support of the hammock.

Why choose Aerial Yoga?

  • Boosts proprioception for better body awareness, balance, and coordination.
  • Deepens stretches for increased flexibility.
  • Strengthens your core like never before.
  • Releases endorphins, fostering a sense of well-being, creativity, and FUN
  • Relieves back pain and spinal compression, promoting back health.
  • Activates the lymphatic system and leads to a better circulation.
  • Enhances Mind-Body Connection.
  • Takes you out of your comfort zone for a fun and intense workout!


Belay/Harness Pose

Surrender to the sway, find strength in suspension, and let the yoga hammock be your portal to a gravity-defying serenity.

The belay/harness aerial yoga pose unveils a mesmerizing dance between body and air. It is a dynamic and visually striking position . It involves a climber suspended min-air, securely attached to the climbing rope via a harness. The pose showcases a harmonious blend of strength, balance, and trust, as the climber relies on both their physical capabilities and the belayer's support!!
