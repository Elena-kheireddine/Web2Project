Kids Yoga

Learn how yoga can help kids improve their physical, mental and emotional well being

Just like the adults Yoga can help kids live more mindfully. It can improve mental health, self-esteem, strengthen the body and improve flexibility.

Yoga is a great way for kids to relax, connect with themselves, and start to better understand their bodies and their emotions.



How do kids benefit from yoga?

  • Reduce stress and anxiety
  • Boost children's concentration and focus
  • Improves their flexibility, strength and balance
  • Build their confidence and self-esteem
  • Promotes healthy eating in children
  • Help children with autism


Our studio provides the best environment for the little yogis offering them a safe space to express themselves and to discover more about their personalities. Along side the fun and joy take a look at how our classes end

  • Making them feel like this is their second home is our number one priority along side with emotional support for everyone and treating them on an equal basis and making sure they never compare their bodies to eachothers. Each one of them is special in their own way.
  • Ending the class with a cute activity and some gifts to make them feel they did a good job and to keep their spirit elevated and positive. Their happiness, mental health and teaching them self-love is what we focus on during the classes.
